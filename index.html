Taylor Swift Era Explorer

A Guide to All of Taylor Swift's Eras

Taylor Swift | Fearless | Speak Now | Red | 1989 | reputation | Lover | folklore | evermore | Midnights | TTPD

Things you should know:

  • Albums that were released during Taylor Swift's record deal with Big Machine Records have or will have a Taylor's Version. This is because Big Machine Records sold the masters (original recordings) of those first six albums, so she does not make money off of them. She signed a deal with Universal's Republic Records in November of 2018, so now she owns her own masters. You can read more about it here.
  • 'The Vault' is where all of the songs that Taylor wrote for an album but didn't get put on the album figuratively are. So, when you a see a single that is 'From the Vault', it was going to be on the album, but then she released it later as a single or in the Taylor's Version of that album. 'From the Vault' songs are still part of the era of the album they were written for. Or, in the case of albums, it just means that it hasn't been released yet. For example, Taylor's Versions that haven't been released yet are in the Vault.
  • This Explorer does not include any of Taylor Swift's Christmas songs/albums, as they don't really belong to any specific era.

Taylor Swift

This era includes the albums 'Taylor Swift' and 'Live From Clear Channel Stripped 2008'.

Taylor Swift, or 'Debut', as her fans like to call it, is Taylor Swift's first album. It was released in 2006, when she was only 17. Its genre is country and its color is green. Its Taylor's Version is currently in the Vault.

Fearless

This era includes the albums 'Fearless', 'Fearless Platinum Edition', 'Fearless (Taylor's Version)', and some singles.

Fearless is Taylor Swift's second album. It was released in 2008. Its genre is country/pop and its color is gold. It has a Taylor's Version.

Speak Now

This era includes the albums 'Speak Now', 'Speak Now (Deluxe Edition)', 'Speak Now World Tour Live', and 'Speak Now (Taylor's Version)'.

Speak Now is Taylor Swift's third album. It was released in 2010. Its genre is country/pop and its color is purple. It has a Taylor's Version.

Red

This era includes the albums 'Red', 'Red (Deluxe Edition)', 'Red (Taylor's Version)', an EP called 'The More Red (Taylor's Version) Chapter', and some singles.

Red is Taylor Swift's fourth album. It was released in 2012. Its genre is country/pop/rock and its color is red (of course). It has a Taylor's Version.

1989

This era includes the albums '1989', '1989 (Deluxe Edition)', '1989 (Taylor's Version)', '1989 (Taylor's Version)[Deluxe]', and some singles.

1989 is Taylor Swift's fifth album. It was released in 2014. Its genre is pop and its color is light blue.

reputation

This era includes the album 'reputation' and some singles.

Reputation (stylized in all lowercase) is Taylor Swift's sixth album. It was released in 2017. Its genre is electro-pop and its color is black. Its Taylor's Version is currently in the Vault.

Lover

This era includes the album 'Lover' and some singles.

Lover is Taylor Swift's seventh album. It was released in 2019. Its genre is pop and its color is pink. It doesn't need a Taylor's Version.

folklore

This era includes the albums 'folklore', 'foklore (deluxe version)', 'folklore: the long pond sessions (from the Disney+special)[deluxe edition]', and some singles.

Folklore (stylized in all lowercase) is Taylor Swift's eighth album. It was released in 2020, and it is the sister album of evermore, which was released 5 months later. Its genre is indie-pop and its color is light grey. It doesn't need a Taylor's Version.

evermore

This era includes the albums 'evermore', 'evermore (deluxe version)', and some singles.

Evermore (stylized in all lowercase) is Taylor Swift's ninth album. It was released in 2020, and it is the sister album of folklore, which was released 5 months earlier. Its genre is folk-pop/alternative rock and its color is a warm brown. It doesn't need a Taylor's Version.

Midnights

This era includes the albums 'Midnights', 'Midnights (3am Edition)', 'Midnights (The Til Dawn Edition)', and some singles.

Midnights is Taylor Swift's tenth album. It was released in 2022. Its genre is electro-pop and its color is (you guessed it) midnight blue. It doesn't need a Taylor's Version.

THE TORTURED POETS DEPARTMENT

This era includes the albums THE TORTURED POETS DEPARTMENT, THE TORTURED POETS DEPARTMENT: THE ANTHOLOGY, and some singles.

THE TORTURED POETS DEPARTMENT (aka TTPD) is Taylor Swift's eleventh album. It was released in 2024. Its genre is chamber/ballad pop and its color is white. TTPD is considered a 'double album'. It doesn't need a Taylor's Version.
